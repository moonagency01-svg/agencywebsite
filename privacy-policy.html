Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This policy explains how Moon Agency ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with this website. It's written to reflect what this site actually does — if that changes (for example, if we add a sign-up form or analytics), this page needs to be updated to match before that change goes live.

On this page

1. Who we are 2. What information we collect 3. How we use information 4. Cookies 5. Third parties & embedded content 6. International data transfers 7. Data retention 8. Your rights 9. Children's privacy 10. Changes to this policy 11. Contact us

1. Who we are

Moon Agency is an official BIGO Live PH agency (PH-MOON2) based in the Philippines. For the purposes of applicable data protection law, Moon Agency is the controller of any personal data processed through this website.

2. What information we collect

This website does not currently include a data-collecting sign-up or application form — host recruitment happens through the BIGO Live app itself. Because of that, direct personal data collection on this site is limited to:

  • Information you send us directly — if you email an address listed on this site, we receive whatever you choose to include (e.g. your name, email address, and message content).
  • Cookie preference — a small record of whether you accepted or rejected non-essential cookies, so we don't ask you again on every visit.
  • Analytics data — if you accept non-essential cookies, Google Analytics collects standard traffic data (pages visited, general location by country/city, device/browser type, how you arrived at the site). This only runs if you accept cookies; rejecting keeps it off entirely.
  • Cookies set by embedded third-party content — see Section 4 below.

We use this analytics data only to understand overall traffic and improve the site — not to identify individual visitors.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries sent to our contact emails
  • To remember your cookie consent choice
  • To display embedded content (tutorial videos) and link out to services (BIGO Live, Google Play) that you choose to interact with

We do not sell personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

This site uses a small number of cookies, described in full in our Cookie Policy. In short: one essential cookie remembers your consent choice, and embedded YouTube videos on the Tutorial page may set their own cookies once you interact with them, depending on your consent choice.

5. Third parties & embedded content

This site links to or embeds content from third parties, including:

  • YouTube (Google) — for tutorial video playback
  • Google Analytics (Google) — for site traffic statistics, only if you accept cookies
  • Google Play — for our Android game listings
  • Google Sheets / Apps Script — used to display publicly-relevant host and PK event schedule information on this site; this reflects our own internal data, not information submitted by site visitors
  • BIGO Live — the livestreaming platform hosts operate on

These third parties operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review. We aren't responsible for how they handle data once you leave this site or interact with their embedded content.

6. International data transfers

Where third-party services above (such as Google/YouTube) process data, that data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own, including the United States. Those providers maintain their own safeguards for international transfers under applicable law.

7. Data retention

Emails sent to our contact addresses are retained only as long as needed to handle your inquiry. Your cookie consent preference is retained for up to 12 months, or until you clear it.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have rights over your personal data. We honor these regardless of location as a matter of practice.

If you're in the European Economic Area (GDPR)

  • Right to access, correct, or erase your data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent (e.g. for cookies) at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

If you're in California (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to know what personal information we hold about you
  • Right to request deletion
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information — we don't sell or share personal information, so there's nothing to opt out of today
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights

If you're in the Philippines (Data Privacy Act, RA 10173)

  • Right to be informed about how your data is processed
  • Right to access and correct your data
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to erasure or blocking of data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 11.

9. Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children, and BIGO Live hosts must be 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site changes. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

11. Contact us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, email our manager at 1234@gmail.com.

This policy is provided as general information and is not legal advice. Moon Agency should have this document reviewed by a qualified lawyer familiar with the jurisdictions its visitors and applicants are located in before relying on it.
